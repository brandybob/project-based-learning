PACKAGES

  • Platinum Package

    • Combines advanced swimming with specialized disciplines like water polo or synchronized swimming. Benefits: Mastery of survival and rescue skills alongside elite-level technique; often the highest award achievable in a program.

  • Gold Package

    • Focuses on refining technique, speed, and stamina. Benefits: Ability to swim 400m within 8 minutes, perform advanced somersaults, and master complex sculling (head and feet first).

  • Bronze Package

    • Focuses on gaining basic water safety and introducing competitive strokes. Benefits: Learn to swim 100m using two strokes, tread water for 1 minute, and perform basic surface dives.
